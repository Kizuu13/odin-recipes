Three Bean Tacos

Are you ready to shit your pants? If you answered yes then step this way and see how to make the most delicous three bean taco you done ever seen.

ingredients

  • 1 (16 ounce) can B&M® Original Baked Beans
  • 1 (15.5 ounce) can Joan of Arc® Light Red Kidney Beans, rinsed and drained
  • 1 (15 ounce) can Joan of Arc® Black Beans, rinsed and drained
  • 1 (11 ounce) can Green Giant® SteamCrisp® Mexicorn®
  • 1 (1.25 ounce) package Ortega® Taco Seasoning Mix
  • ¾ cup water
  • 12 Ortega® flour tortillas, warmed
  • Queso fresco, lettuce, and red onion for topping

Directions

  1. Add beans, Green Giant® SteamCrisp® Mexicorn®, 3/4 cup water, and Ortega® Taco Seasoning to large nonstick skillet and bring to a boil over high heat. Simmer 10 minutes or until thickened.
  2. Serve in warm tortillas with your favorite taco toppings.

Nutrition Facts

Per Serving: 239 calories; protein 4.2g; carbohydrates 45.8g; fat 2.1g; sodium 732.9mg
